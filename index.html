Download APK

First time? Android will ask you to allow installs from this app/browser. Tap Settings → Allow, then open the downloaded file.

Installing apps on Android

  1. Tap an app, then Download APK.
  2. When the download finishes, tap the file (or open it from your notifications).
  3. The first time, Android asks permission to install unknown apps — toggle it on for your browser.
  4. Tap Install. Done! The app lands in your app drawer.

This is normal “sideloading.” These are your own apps, so it’s safe to allow.
